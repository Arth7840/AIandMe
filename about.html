Why AIandMe?

We have configured the AI for the Indian education field. Our AI is trained on CBSE or state syllabus for 11th and 12th based on the NCERT/state books and CBSE syllabus. It can bring chapters and sub-chapters of the CBSE books. It can adjust/answer based on the competency level of the students. The competency can be defined as Novice, Advanced Beginner, Competent, Proficient and Expert.

It can generate study plans and exercises based on the syllabus. It can also evaluate the students on the generated exercises including written questions. We also plan to add a module for teachers where they can improve their competency. We appreciate the fact that teachers also have to go through their career progression and have aspirations of becoming Principals.
